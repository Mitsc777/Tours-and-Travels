Itinerary
DayPlaceThings to do
1SingaporeArrive at Singapore. On arrival visit Gardens by the bay - A Spanning 101 hectares, this superpark is home to over a quarter of a million rare plants. Visit Flower Dome, Cloud Forest Dome, take a walk on the skyway and enjoy the sound and light showat The Supertree grove. Notes : No Light & Sound show at Gardens by the Bay from 02 Dec'16 â€“ 01 Jan'17 due to Annual Christmas Wonderland.
2SingaporeCity orientation tour includes the Parliament House, Suntec City, Fountain of Wealth, City Hall, Orchard Road and Swiss-the tallest hotel in Singapore. the statue of Sir Thomas Stamford Raffles. Enjoy Singapore river boat ride and take a photo stop at Merlion. Free time for shopping at Little India. Experience the Cable Car ride from Mt. Faber to Sentosa Island. Visit Madame Tussaud's which displays an array of wax sculptures & other wax works along with Images of Singapore. Visit Marine Life Park - the largest oceanarium in the world, which has the world's largest viewing panel and contains 100,000 marine animals of over 800 species. Enjoy the 'Wings of Time' - a spectacular night show set outdoors against the backdrop of an open sea in the evening. Take a mono-rail ride.
3SingaporeExperience the Singapore Flyer ride offering the breathtaking panorama of the shimmering Marina Bay waterfront and historical landmarks dotting the city skyline. Visit Universal studios at Resorts World to enjoy various activities at Lost World, FarFar Away, Madagascar, Hollywood, New York, Sci fi city & Ancient Egypt.
4Singapore-Malaysia-Genting HighlandsProceed to Genting Highlands by coach. En-route visit to Batu Caves. Travel by Asia's longest and fastest Cable car to Genting Highlands. Try your luck at famous Casino in the evening. Please Note: Pack a separate handbag for Genting Highlands. Light woollens are required at Genting. Casino dress code is formals, Men - Trouser, Collar Shirt / T-shirt, Shoes; Women - Saree, Punjabi or western formal. Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner
5Kuala Lumpur-Genting HighlandsEnjoy Indoor Theme Park alongwith Snow World and Ripley's Believe it or not. Enjoy shopping at Genting. Later proceed to Kuala Lumpur by coach. Arrive at Kuala Lumpur. Enjoy photo stop at Petronas Twin Towers, Visit to Suria KLCC which located at thefeet of the Petronas Twin Towers. During city tour view King's Palace, Dataran Merdeka Square, National Mosque, Cricket Field, High Court Building. Enjoy magnificent view of city from the Observatory Deck of the K. L. Tower. Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner
6Kuala lumpurVisit Sunway Lagoon. Enjoy the most spectacular adventure and never ending excitement of rides and water slides at Sunway Lagoon's - Waters of Africa. Free time for shopping at Sungie Wang Plaza. Visit Putrajaya - the Administrative capital of Malaysia. Please Note: Carry round neck T-shirt and shorts / short or long tights / swimsuits with towel for Sunway Lagoon water rides.
7Kuala Lumpur-Bangkok-PatayaFlight to Bangkok. On arrival proceed to Pattaya by coach. Visit Nong Nooch Village for Elephant show, Thai Cultural Show, Tropical Garden, Orchid Garden and Pottery Garden. Enjoy Spectacular Alcazar show in the evening.
8PattayaEnjoy Speed Boat Ride, Para sailing, Coral formations by Glass Bottom Boat and swimming in the crystal clear blue waters of Pattaya at Coral Island. Enjoy Pattaya Park tower which holds the real wonder of showcasing a view of the entire city from its55th floor and also enjoy tower jump / cable ride at the tower. Relax yourself with Scientific Thai Massage.
9Pattaya-BangkokProceed to Bangkok by coach. En-route visit the World's biggest Gems Gallery. On arrival in the evening enjoy Chao Phraya River Cruise with dinner, dance and live music.
10BangkokCity tour includes the world's largest Buddha - Golden Buddha followed by Principle Buddha and Reclining Buddha. Board the flight for your hometown. Tour concludes.
