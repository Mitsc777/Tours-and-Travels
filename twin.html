Standing majestically at a height of 451.9 metres, Petronas Twin Towers is a world-renowned icon of Malaysia.
This gleaming structure of steel and glass is the centre piece of the Kuala Lumpur City Centre or KLCC, which consists of a shopping mall,
star-rated hotels, a landscaped park and a convention centre.
The highlight of a visit to the Twin Towers is the exhilaration of standing at the Skybridge on the 41st Floor and Viewing Deck on the 86th Floor.
